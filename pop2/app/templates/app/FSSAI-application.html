FSSAI BASIC REGISTRATION

FSSAI Basic Registration refers to the process of obtaining basic registration under the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). FSSAI is a regulatory body responsible for ensuring the safety and quality of food products in India. Basic registration is applicable to small food businesses that have an annual turnover of up to Rs. 12 lakhs.

The FSSAI Basic Registration is a simplified and streamlined procedure that aims to bring such small-scale food businesses under the regulatory framework. It involves providing basic information about the business and its activities to ensure compliance with food safety regulations.

By obtaining FSSAI Basic Registration, food businesses demonstrate their commitment to maintaining food safety standards and ensuring the well-being of consumers. It is a legal requirement for small food businesses to ensure the safety and quality of the food products they manufacture or sell.

The FSSAI Basic Registration process typically involves filling out an application form with details such as the name and address of the business, the type of food products involved, and basic contact information. After successful registration, the business is issued a unique 14-digit FSSAI registration number, which needs to be displayed on food packages and promotional materials.

Overall, FSSAI Basic Registration plays a crucial role in promoting food safety and consumer protection. It encourages small food businesses to operate in a responsible and regulated manner, ensuring the availability of safe and hygienic food products in the market.

FEATURES OF FSSAI BASIC REGISTRATION

  1. Legal Compliance: FSSAI Basic Registration ensures that small food businesses comply with the legal requirements set forth by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. It is mandatory for businesses involved in the manufacturing, storage, distribution, or sale of food products.
  2. Entry-Level Registration: FSSAI Basic Registration is specifically designed for small-scale food businesses with an annual turnover of up to Rs. 12 lakhs. It provides a simplified and streamlined registration process tailored to the needs of such businesses.
  3. FSSAI Registration Number: After successful registration, businesses receive a unique 14-digit FSSAI registration number. This number serves as an identity for the business and must be displayed on food packages and promotional materials.
  4. Compliance with Food Safety Standards: FSSAI Basic Registration requires businesses to adhere to the food safety standards and regulations set by FSSAI. It ensures that the food products manufactured or sold by the business are safe for consumption and meet the necessary quality parameters.
  5. Recognition and Credibility: FSSAI Basic Registration enhances the credibility and recognition of small food businesses. It establishes their compliance with food safety regulations, which can be a significant factor in building trust among consumers, suppliers, and business partners.
  6. Simplified Registration Process: FSSAI Basic Registration offers a simplified and user-friendly registration process, making it easier for small food businesses to comply with the regulatory requirements. The process can be completed online through the FSSAI portal, reducing administrative burden and paperwork.

BENEFITS OF FSSAI BASIC REGISTRATION

  1. Legal Compliance: FSSAI Basic Registration ensures that businesses operating in the food sector comply with the legal requirements set by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. It helps businesses avoid penalties and legal issues associated with non-compliance.
  2. Consumer Trust and Confidence: FSSAI Basic Registration enhances consumer trust and confidence in the food products being sold. It assures consumers that the products meet the necessary safety and quality standards prescribed by FSSAI, leading to increased customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  3. Market Access and Expansion: FSSAI Basic Registration provides businesses with access to a wider market. Many retail establishments, distributors, and e-commerce platforms require FSSAI registration as a prerequisite for selling food products. Registration opens doors to new sales channels and opportunities for business expansion.
  4. Improved Business Credibility: FSSAI Basic Registration enhances the credibility and reputation of small food businesses. It serves as proof of compliance with food safety standards and distinguishes registered businesses from unregistered ones, instilling confidence among consumers, suppliers, and business partners.
  5. Competitive Advantage: FSSAI Basic Registration provides a competitive advantage in the market. It demonstrates a commitment to food safety and quality, setting registered businesses apart from their non-registered counterparts. This differentiation can attract more customers and give businesses an edge over the competition.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR FSSAI BASIC REGISTRATION

Following are the commonly required documents for FSSAI Basic Registration:

  1. Identity Proof: Documents such as Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, PAN card, or Passport of the proprietor, partner, or director of the business, depending on the business type.
  2. Address Proof: Documents such as Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, Passport, or utility bills (electricity bill, water bill, etc.) displaying the address of the business premises.
  3. Photograph: Recent passport-sized photographs of the proprietor, partner, or director of the business.
  4. Business Entity Proof: Documents related to the business entity, such as Partnership Deed for partnership firms, Memorandum and Articles of Association for companies, or Certificate of Incorporation for LLPs (Limited Liability Partnerships).
  5. Business Premises Proof: Documents providing proof of ownership or legal possession of the business premises, such as property documents, lease agreement, or rental agreement.
  6. Food Safety Management System Plan: For certain categories of businesses, a Food Safety Management System (FSMS) plan may be required, outlining the food safety practices, procedures, and protocols implemented by the business.
  7. Declaration Form: A self-declaration form indicating compliance with the requirements and regulations prescribed by FSSAI.

PROCESS FOR FSSAI BASIC REGISTRATION

The process for FSSAI Basic Registration involves the following steps:

  1. Determine Eligibility: Ensure that your business qualifies for FSSAI Basic Registration. It is applicable to small food businesses with an annual turnover of up to Rs. 12 lakhs.
  2. Gather Required Documents: Collect the necessary documents as mentioned in the previous response. These may include identity proof, address proof, business entity proof, business premises proof, photographs, and any other specific documents required for your business category.
  3. Online Registration: Visit the official FSSAI website or the Food Licensing and Registration System (FLRS) portal (https://foodlicensing.fssai.gov.in/) to initiate the registration process.
  4. Create an Account: Create an account on the FLRS portal by providing your basic details and creating a username and password.
  5. Fill the Application Form: Log in to your account and fill in the FSSAI Basic Registration application form. Provide accurate and complete information about your business, including business name, address, contact details, food category, and type of business.
  6. Upload Documents: Upload the required documents as per the guidelines provided on the portal. Ensure that the documents are clear, legible, and in the specified format (such as PDF or JPEG).
  7. Fee Payment: Pay the requisite fee for FSSAI Basic Registration. The fee amount may vary depending on the type and scale of your business.
  8. Submit Application: Review the filled-in application form and uploaded documents for accuracy. Once verified, submit the application on the portal.
  9. Application Processing: The FSSAI authorities will review and process your application. They may contact you for any clarifications or additional information if required.
  10. Registration Certificate: Upon successful processing of your application, you will receive an FSSAI Basic Registration Certificate. This certificate will contain your unique FSSAI registration number.
  11. Display FSSAI Logo: After receiving the registration certificate, you can display the FSSAI logo and your registration number on your food product labels, packaging, or promotional materials.

It's important to note that the FSSAI Basic Registration process may be subject to updates and changes. It is advisable to refer to the official FSSAI website or consult with relevant authorities for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the process of FSSAI Basic Registration.

FAQS

Q.1. What is FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: FSSAI Basic Registration is the process of obtaining a basic registration certificate from the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) for small food businesses.

Q.2. Who is eligible for FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: Small food businesses with an annual turnover of up to Rs. 12 lakhs are eligible for FSSAI Basic Registration.

Q.3. What are the benefits of FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: Benefits include legal compliance, enhanced consumer trust, market access, improved credibility, and compliance with food safety standards.

Q.4. How long does it take to get FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: The registration process can usually be completed within 7-10 days, depending on the verification process and documentation.

Q.5. Is FSSAI Basic Registration mandatory?
Ans: Yes, FSSAI Basic Registration is mandatory for small food businesses as per the Food Safety and Standards Act.

Q.6. How can I apply for FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: You can apply for FSSAI Basic Registration online through the Food Licensing and Registration System (FLRS) portal of FSSAI.

Q.7. What documents are required for FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: Documents such as identity proof, address proof, business entity proof, business premises proof, and photographs are typically required.

Q.8. How long is the FSSAI Basic Registration valid?
Ans: FSSAI Basic Registration is valid for a period of one to five years, depending on the selected registration tenure.

Q.9. Can I upgrade from FSSAI Basic Registration to a higher category?
Ans: Yes, if your business exceeds the turnover limit for FSSAI Basic Registration, you can upgrade to a higher category of registration.

Q.10. Can I sell my food products without FSSAI Basic Registration?
Ans: No, it is illegal to sell food products without obtaining the necessary FSSAI registration, including FSSAI Basic Registration for eligible businesses.
